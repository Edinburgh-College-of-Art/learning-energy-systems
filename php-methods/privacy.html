Privacy Policy

The Energy Diary app does not request any personal information from users. Therefore, all data recorded through the app is done so anonymously. Further, all data recorded in the app is gathered by subjective observation, and may not represent the schools actual electricity consumption.

All data recorded and received by us from users of the Learning Energy Systems app, including school names and observed usage data, is stored in a secure database. The data is not shared with any third parties, unless we are required to do so to comply with any legal or regulatory obligation or request. We will do our best to prevent unauthorised access.
